About A Green Field Public School

Established with a vision to provide quality education, A Green Field Public School has been a pillar of academic excellence in our community.

Our school is dedicated to nurturing young minds from Kindergarten through 6th grade, providing a strong foundation for lifelong learning. We believe in a holistic approach to education that combines academic rigor with character development.

Our Academic Programs

Comprehensive education from Kindergarten to 6th Grade

Kindergarten

Foundation learning with focus on social skills, basic literacy, and numeracy through play-based activities.

  • Phonics & Reading
  • Basic Mathematics
  • Art & Craft
  • Social Skills

Grades 1-2

Building fundamental skills with interactive learning approaches and creative expression.

  • Reading & Writing
  • Mathematics
  • Science Discovery
  • Physical Education

Grades 3-4

Expanding knowledge with structured curriculum and project-based learning.

  • English & Literature
  • Mathematics
  • Science & Social Studies
  • Computer Basics

Grades 5-6

Preparing for middle school with advanced concepts and critical thinking skills.

  • Advanced Mathematics
  • Science (Physics, Biology)
  • Social Studies
  • Research Skills
